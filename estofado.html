Estofado


Ingredients:
  • 500 grams pork belly, cut into cubes
    		•
  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
    		•
  • 1 medium red onion, chopped
    		•
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
    		•
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
    		•
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
    		•
  • 2 bay leaves
    		•
  • 1 cup water
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Sauté the aromatics
In a pot, sauté garlic, onions, and tomatoes until softened.

Step 2 - Cook the pork
Add the pork belly and cook until lightly browned.

Step 3 - Add the liquid ingredients
Pour in soy sauce, vinegar, and water. Add brown sugar and bay leaves. Simmer until the pork is tender.

Step 4 - Season
Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot with rice.
