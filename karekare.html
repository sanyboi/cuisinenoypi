Kare Kare


Ingredients:
  • 1 kg oxtail, cut into serving pieces
    		•
  • 1/2 kg tripe, cleaned
    		•
  • 1 bunch pechay (bok choy)
    		•
  • 1 bunch sitaw (string beans), cut into 2-inch pieces
    		•
  • 1 banana heart, sliced
    		•
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
    		•
  • 1/4 cup ground toasted rice
    		•
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 1 medium red onion, chopped
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons annatto seeds
    		•
  • 1/4 cup shrimp paste (bagoong alamang)
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Boil the meat
In a large pot, boil the oxtail and tripe until tender. Set aside the broth.

Step 2 - Sauté the aromatics
In a separate pan, heat oil and sauté garlic and onions until fragrant. Add the annatto seeds for color, then remove the seeds.

Step 3 - Add the peanut butter and toasted rice
Add peanut butter and ground toasted rice to the pan. Stir until well mixed.

Step 4 - Combine the meat and broth
Pour the mixture into the pot with the boiled meat. Add the broth and simmer until the sauce thickens.

Step 5 - Add the vegetables
Add the pechay, sitaw, and banana heart. Cook until vegetables are tender.

Step 6 - Serve
Serve hot with shrimp paste on the side. Enjoy your Kare Kare!
