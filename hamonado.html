Hamonado


Ingredients:
  • 1 kg pork belly, sliced into 1-inch thick pieces
    		•
  • 1 cup pineapple juice
    		•
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
    		•
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
    		•
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
    		•
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
    		•
  • 1 medium red onion, chopped
    		•
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 2 bay leaves
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Marinate the pork
In a bowl, combine pineapple juice, soy sauce, brown sugar, and vinegar. Marinate the pork belly slices in this mixture for at least 1 hour.

Step 2 - Sauté the aromatics
In a pot, sauté garlic and onions until fragrant.

Step 3 - Cook the pork
Add the marinated pork belly along with the marinade. Add bay leaves. Bring to a boil and simmer until the pork is tender.

Step 4 - Add pineapple chunks
Add the pineapple chunks and cook for another 10 minutes.

Step 5 - Season
Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot with rice.
