Daing na Bangus


Ingredients:
  • 1 whole bangus (milkfish), cleaned and butterflied
    		•
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
    		•
  • 1 head garlic, minced
    		•
  • 1 teaspoon salt
    		•
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
    		•
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground peppercorns
    		•
  • Oil for frying
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Marinate the Fish
In a bowl, combine the vinegar, garlic, salt, and ground black pepper. Mix well. Place the bangus in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over it. Make sure the fish is well-coated. Marinate for at least 1 hour or overnight in the refrigerator.

Step 2 - Preheat the Oil
In a large skillet, heat the oil over medium heat.

Step 3 - Fry the Fish
Remove the bangus from the marinade and let any excess liquid drip off. Fry the fish, skin side first, until golden brown and crispy, about 4-5 minutes per side.

Step 4 - Drain and Serve
Remove the fish from the skillet and let it drain on paper towels to remove excess oil. Transfer to a serving plate.

Step 5 - Serve
Serve the Daing na Bangus hot with steamed rice and a dipping sauce of vinegar with crushed garlic and chili. Enjoy your delicious Daing na Bangus!
