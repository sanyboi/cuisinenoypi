Balbacua


Ingredients:
  • 1 kg beef, cut into chunks
    		•
  • 1/2 kg ox tail
    		•
  • 1/4 kg ox tripe
    		•
  • 1 onion, chopped
    		•
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, sliced
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons annatto powder (achuete)
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons oil
    		•
  • 1 cup water
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Sauté the Aromatics
In a large pot, heat the oil over medium heat. Sauté the garlic, onion, and ginger until fragrant.

Step 2 - Add the Beef and Ox Tail
Add the beef chunks and ox tail to the pot and cook until browned.

Step 3 - Add the Ox Tripe and Annatto Powder
Add the ox tripe and annatto powder. Stir well to coat the meat.

Step 4 - Add Water and Peanut Butter
Pour in the water and add the peanut butter. Stir until the peanut butter is dissolved.

Step 5 - Simmer Until Tender
Cover the pot and simmer for about 2 to 3 hours or until the meat is tender. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 6 - Serve
Serve hot with steamed rice. Enjoy your delicious Balbacua!
