Aligue


Ingredients:
  • 1/2 cup crab fat (aligue)
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons butter
    		•
  • 1 head garlic, minced
    		•
  • 1 onion, chopped
    		•
  • 1/2 cup coconut cream
    		•
  • 1 tablespoon lemon or calamansi juice
    		•
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    		•
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Sauté the Aromatics
In a large pan, heat the butter over medium heat. Add the onions and garlic and sauté until they are translucent.

Step 2 - Add the Aligue
Add the crab fat and cook for a few minutes until it is well combined with the aromatics.

Step 3 - Add the Coconut Cream
Add the coconut cream and simmer for 5 minutes until the sauce has thickened.

Step 4 - Add the Lemon Juice
Add the lemon or calamansi juice and season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir well to combine.

Step 5 - Serve
Serve the aligue hot with steamed rice. Enjoy your delicious Aligue!
