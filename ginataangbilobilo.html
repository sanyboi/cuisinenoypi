Ginataang Bilo-Bilo


Ingredients:
  • 2 cups glutinous rice flour
    		•
  • 1 cup water
    		•
  • 2 cups coconut milk
    		•
  • 1 cup sugar
    		•
  • 1 cup sweet potatoes, cubed
    		•
  • 1 cup saba bananas, sliced
    		•
  • 1 cup jackfruit, sliced
    		•
  • 1/2 cup tapioca pearls, cooked
    		•
  • 2 cups water (for cooking)
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Prepare the Bilo-Bilo
In a bowl, mix the glutinous rice flour and water to form a dough. Shape into small balls (bilo-bilo) and set aside.

Step 2 - Cook the coconut milk
In a pot, bring the coconut milk and water to a boil. Add sugar and stir until dissolved.

Step 3 - Add the vegetables and fruits
Add the sweet potatoes and cook until tender. Then add the saba bananas, jackfruit, and bilo-bilo. Cook until the bilo-bilo floats.

Step 4 - Add the tapioca pearls
Add the cooked tapioca pearls and simmer for another 5 minutes. Serve hot or cold.
