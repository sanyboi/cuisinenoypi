Lechon Kawali


Ingredients:
  • 1 kg pork belly (liempo)
    		•
  • 1 tablespoon salt
    		•
  • 1 tablespoon whole peppercorns
    		•
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
    		•
  • 2 bay leaves
    		•
  • Water for boiling
    		•
  • Oil for frying
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Boil the pork belly
In a large pot, combine pork belly, salt, peppercorns, garlic, bay leaves, and enough water to cover the pork. Boil for 1-2 hours or until the pork is tender.

Step 2 - Dry the pork belly
Remove the pork from the pot and let it cool. Pat dry with paper towels to remove excess moisture.

Step 3 - Fry the pork belly
Heat oil in a deep frying pan. Carefully add the pork belly and fry until golden brown and crispy. Remove from oil and drain on paper towels.

Step 4 - Slice and serve
Slice the lechon kawali into serving pieces and serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce. Enjoy your Lechon Kawali!
