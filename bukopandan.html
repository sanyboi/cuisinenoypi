Buko Pandan


Ingredients:
  • 2 cups young coconut meat (buko), shredded
    		•
  • 2 packs (90g each) green gelatin powder
    		•
  • 2 cups coconut water
    		•
  • 1 cup condensed milk
    		•
  • 1 cup heavy cream
    		•
  • 1/2 cup sugar
    		•
  • 1 teaspoon pandan extract
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Prepare the Gelatin
In a pot, combine the gelatin powder, coconut water, and sugar. Stir well and bring to a boil over medium heat. Add the pandan extract and mix thoroughly. Pour the mixture into a mold and let it cool until set. Cut into small cubes once firm.

Step 2 - Mix the Ingredients
In a large mixing bowl, combine the shredded young coconut, gelatin cubes, condensed milk, and heavy cream. Mix well until all ingredients are evenly distributed.

Step 3 - Chill
Transfer the mixture to a serving dish or individual cups. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 1-2 hours before serving.

Step 4 - Serve
Serve the Buko Pandan chilled. Enjoy your delicious Buko Pandan!
