Binagoongan


Ingredients:
  • 1 kg pork belly, cut into cubes
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons oil
    		•
  • 1 head garlic, minced
    		•
  • 1 onion, chopped
    		•
  • 4 tablespoons shrimp paste (bagoong)
    		•
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
    		•
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
    		•
  • 1/4 cup water
    		•
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Sauté the Aromatics
In a large pot, heat the oil over medium heat. Add the garlic and onion and sauté until they are translucent.

Step 2 - Cook the Pork
Add the pork belly and cook until it is browned on all sides.

Step 3 - Add the Shrimp Paste
Add the shrimp paste and tomatoes to the pot. Cook for a few minutes until the tomatoes are soft.

Step 4 - Simmer
Add the vinegar and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 20-30 minutes or until the pork is tender.

Step 5 - Season
Add the sugar, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Continue to simmer until the sauce has thickened to your liking.

Step 6 - Serve
Serve the Binagoongan hot with steamed rice. Enjoy your delicious Binagoongan!
