Arroz Caldo


Ingredients:
  • 1 cup glutinous rice
    		•
  • 1/2 kg chicken, cut into pieces
    		•
  • 1 head garlic, minced
    		•
  • 1 onion, chopped
    		•
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger, sliced
    		•
  • 6 cups chicken broth
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs, sliced
    		•
  • Spring onions, chopped (for garnish)
    		•
  • Calamansi or lemon (optional)
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Sauté the Aromatics
In a large pot, heat some oil over medium heat. Add the garlic, onion, and ginger and sauté until fragrant.

Step 2 - Add the Chicken
Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are lightly browned.

Step 3 - Add the Rice
Add the glutinous rice and stir to coat it
