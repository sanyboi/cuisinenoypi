Batchoy


Ingredients:
  • 500g miki noodles
    		•
  • 200g pork belly, sliced
    		•
  • 100g pork liver, sliced
    		•
  • 1 onion, chopped
    		•
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 1/2 cup chicharon, crushed
    		•
  • 1/2 cup shrimp, peeled and deveined
    		•
  • 6 cups pork broth
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Sauté the Aromatics
In a large pot, heat the oil over medium heat. Sauté the garlic and onion until fragrant.

Step 2 - Cook the Meat and Shrimp
Add the pork belly and cook until browned. Add the pork liver and shrimp, cooking for another 2 minutes.

Step 3 - Add the Broth
Pour in the pork broth and soy sauce. Bring to a boil.

Step 4 - Add the Noodles
Add the miki noodles and cook until tender.

Step 5 - Serve
Serve hot with crushed chicharon on top. Enjoy your delicious Batchoy!
