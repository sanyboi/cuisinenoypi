Hardinera


Ingredients:
  • 1 kg pork shoulder, cubed
    		•
  • 1/2 cup liver spread
    		•
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
    		•
  • 1/2 cup raisins
    		•
  • 1/2 cup sweet pickles, chopped
    		•
  • 1/2 cup bell pepper, chopped
    		•
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese
    		•
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
    		•
  • 1/4 cup tomato sauce
    		•
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
    		•
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
  • 4 eggs, beaten
    		•
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Sauté the aromatics
In a pan, sauté garlic and onions until fragrant. Add the pork and cook until lightly browned.

Step 2 - Add the ingredients
Add liver spread, pineapple chunks, raisins, sweet pickles, bell pepper, grated cheese, soy sauce, and tomato sauce. Season with salt and pepper. Simmer for 15 minutes.

Step 3 - Combine with eggs and breadcrumbs
In a bowl, combine the cooked mixture with beaten eggs and breadcrumbs. Mix well.

Step 4 - Steam the hardinera
Transfer the mixture to a llanera (baking mold) lined with banana leaves or foil. Steam for 1 hour or until set.

Step 5 - Serve
Let cool before removing from the mold. Slice and serve. Enjoy your Hardinera!
