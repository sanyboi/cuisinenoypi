Ginisang Ampalaya


Ingredients:
  • 2 pieces ampalaya (bitter melon), sliced thinly
    		•
  • 1 medium red onion, chopped
    		•
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
    		•
  • 2 eggs, beaten
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Sauté the aromatics
In a pan, sauté garlic, onions, and tomatoes until softened.

Step 2 - Add the ampalaya
Add the sliced ampalaya and cook until tender.

Step 3 - Add the eggs
Pour in the beaten eggs and stir until cooked.

Step 4 - Season
Add fish sauce, salt, and pepper to taste. Serve hot with rice.
