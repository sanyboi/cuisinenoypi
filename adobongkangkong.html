Adobong Kangkong


Ingredients:
  • 1 bunch water spinach (kangkong), cut into 2-inch lengths
    		•
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
    		•
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
    		•
  • 1 head garlic, minced
    		•
  • 1 onion, chopped
    		•
  • 2 bay leaves
    		•
  • 1 teaspoon peppercorns
    		•
  • 1/4 cup oil
    		•
  • 1 cup water
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Sauté the Aromatics
In a large pot, heat the oil over medium heat. Add the onions and garlic and sauté until they are translucent.

Step 2 - Add the Kangkong
Add the water spinach and cook for a few minutes until they are slightly tender.

Step 3 - Simmer the Adobo
Add the soy sauce, vinegar, water, bay leaves, and peppercorns to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 10-15 minutes until the spinach is tender and the sauce has reduced.

Step 4 - Adjust Seasoning
Season with salt and pepper to taste. Continue to simmer until the sauce has thickened to your liking.

Step 5 - Serve
Serve the adobong kangkong hot with steamed rice. Enjoy your delicious Adobong Kangkong!
