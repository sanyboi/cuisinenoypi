Mango Float


Ingredients:
  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
    		•
  • 1 cup condensed milk
    		•
  • 1 cup all-purpose cream
    		•
  • 4 ripe mangoes, sliced
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Prepare the cream mixture
In a bowl, combine condensed milk and all-purpose cream. Mix well until smooth.

Step 2 - Layer the ingredients
In a rectangular dish, layer graham cracker crumbs, followed by a layer of the cream mixture, and then a layer of sliced mangoes. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of mangoes on top.

Step 3 - Chill
Cover the dish with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight to allow the flavors to meld and the layers to set.

Step 4 - Serve
Slice and serve cold. Enjoy your Mango Float!
