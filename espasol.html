Espasol


Ingredients:
  • 2 cups glutinous rice flour
    		•
  • 1 cup coconut milk
    		•
  • 1 cup sugar
    		•
  • 1/2 cup toasted rice flour
    		•
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    		•
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Toast the Rice Flour
In a skillet over medium heat, toast the glutinous rice flour until it turns light brown and smells fragrant. Stir continuously to prevent burning. Set aside.

Step 2 - Mix the Wet Ingredients
In a saucepan, combine the coconut milk, sugar, vanilla extract, and salt. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the sugar has dissolved completely.

Step 3 - Combine Ingredients
Slowly add the toasted glutinous rice flour to the coconut milk mixture, stirring continuously to avoid lumps. Cook until the mixture thickens and becomes dough-like.

Step 4 - Shape the Espasol
Remove the mixture from heat and let it cool slightly. Dust a clean surface with toasted rice flour. Take small portions of the dough and roll them into cylindrical shapes. Roll each piece in the toasted rice flour to coat.

Step 5 - Serve
Let the Espasol pieces cool completely before serving. Enjoy this sweet and chewy Filipino delicacy!
