Lumpiang Sariwa


Ingredients:
  • 2 cups julienned carrots
    		•
  • 2 cups julienned green beans
    		•
  • 2 cups shredded cabbage
    		•
  • 1 cup julienned sweet potatoes
    		•
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
    		•
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
    		•
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    		•
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
    		•
  • 1/2 cup water
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
  • Oil for sautéing
    		•
Wrapper Ingredients:
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
    		•
  • 2 cups water
    		•
  • 2 eggs, beaten
    		•
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
    		•
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    		•
Peanut Sauce Ingredients:
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
    		•
  • 1/2 cup water
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
    		•
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
    		•
  • 1/4 cup ground peanuts for topping
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Sauté the aromatics
In a large pan, heat oil over medium heat. Sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.

Step 2 - Cook the vegetables
Add the julienned sweet potatoes and cook until slightly tender. Add the carrots, green beans, and cabbage. Stir-fry for a few minutes.

Step 3 - Add seasonings and sprouts
Pour in soy sauce, fish sauce, and water. Add the bean sprouts and cook until all the vegetables are tender. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Set aside to cool.

Step 4 - Make the wrapper
In a bowl, combine flour, water, beaten eggs, cooking oil, and salt. Mix until smooth. Heat a non-stick pan and pour a thin layer of the batter to make a crepe. Cook until set. Repeat with remaining batter.

Step 5 - Make the peanut sauce
In a saucepan, combine peanut butter, water, soy sauce, brown sugar, and garlic. Cook over low heat until smooth and thick. Adjust consistency with water if needed. Set aside.

Step 6 - Assemble the Lumpiang Sariwa
Place a wrapper on a plate, add a portion of the cooked vegetables, and roll tightly. Repeat with remaining wrappers and filling.

Step 7 - Serve
Serve with peanut sauce and top with ground peanuts. Enjoy your Lumpiang Sariwa!
