Biko


Ingredients:
  • 2 cups glutinous rice
    		•
  • 1 1/2 cups water
    		•
  • 2 cups coconut milk
    		•
  • 1 cup brown sugar
    		•
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    		•
  • Banana leaves (for lining)
    		•
  • 1/2 cup coconut cream (for topping)
    		•
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar (for topping)
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Cook the Rice
In a large pot, combine the glutinous rice and water. Cook over medium heat until the rice is half-cooked.

Step 2 - Prepare the Coconut Mixture
In another pot, combine the coconut milk, brown sugar, and salt. Cook over medium heat, stirring continuously until the mixture thickens and becomes syrupy.

Step 3 - Mix Rice and Coconut Syrup
Add the half-cooked rice to the coconut mixture. Stir continuously until the rice is fully cooked and the mixture is well combined.

Step 4 - Transfer to Pan
Line a baking dish with banana leaves. Transfer the cooked rice mixture to the lined dish and spread it evenly.

Step 5 - Prepare Topping
In a small pot, combine the coconut cream and brown sugar. Cook over medium heat until thick and syrupy. Pour this mixture over the cooked rice in the baking dish.

Step 6 - Bake
Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Bake the Biko for about 20-30 minutes or until the topping is set and slightly caramelized.

Step 7 - Serve
Let the Biko cool before slicing into squares. Serve and enjoy your delicious Biko!
