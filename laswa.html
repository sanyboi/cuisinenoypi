Laswa


Ingredients:
  • 1 cup squash, cubed
    		•
  • 1 cup eggplant, sliced
    		•
  • 1 cup okra, sliced
    		•
  • 1 cup string beans, cut into 2-inch pieces
    		•
  • 1 cup jute leaves (saluyot)
    		•
  • 1 medium tomato, chopped
    		•
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
    		•
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 2 cups water
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Boil the water
In a pot, bring the water to a boil. Add the onion, garlic, and tomato. Cook until softened.

Step 2 - Add the vegetables
Add the squash and cook until tender. Then add the eggplant, okra, and string beans. Cook until all vegetables are tender.

Step 3 - Add the jute leaves
Add the jute leaves (saluyot) and cook for another 2 minutes.

Step 4 - Season
Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 5 - Serve
Serve hot with rice. Enjoy your Laswa!
