Bulanglang


Ingredients:
  • 2 cups pork belly, cut into cubes
    		•
  • 1 medium onion, quartered
    		•
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons shrimp paste (bagoong)
    		•
  • 2 medium tomatoes, quartered
    		•
  • 2 cups water
    		•
  • 1 small unripe papaya, sliced
    		•
  • 1 small eggplant, sliced
    		•
  • 1 bunch sitaw (string beans), cut into 2-inch pieces
    		•
  • 1 bunch kangkong (water spinach), leaves and tender stems only
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Sauté the Aromatics
In a large pot, sauté the garlic and onion over medium heat until fragrant and translucent.

Step 2 - Add the Pork
Add the pork belly and cook until it is browned on all sides.

Step 3 - Add the Tomatoes and Shrimp Paste
Add the tomatoes and shrimp paste. Cook for a few minutes until the tomatoes are soft.

Step 4 - Simmer
Add the water and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat and let it simmer until the pork is tender.

Step 5 - Add Vegetables
Add the papaya and eggplant. Cook until the vegetables are tender.

Step 6 - Add Remaining Vegetables
Add the sitaw and kangkong. Continue to cook until the vegetables are done to your liking.

Step 7 - Season
Season with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the seasoning as necessary.

Step 8 - Serve
Serve the Bulanglang hot with steamed rice. Enjoy your delicious Bulanglang!
