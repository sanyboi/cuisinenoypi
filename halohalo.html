Halo Halo


Ingredients:
  • 1 cup shaved ice
    		•
  • 1/4 cup sweetened bananas
    		•
  • 1/4 cup sweetened jackfruit
    		•
  • 1/4 cup sweetened red beans
    		•
  • 1/4 cup sweetened white beans
    		•
  • 1/4 cup sweetened garbanzos
    		•
  • 1/4 cup nata de coco
    		•
  • 1/4 cup kaong (sugar palm fruit)
    		•
  • 1/4 cup macapuno (coconut sport)
    		•
  • 1/4 cup leche flan, cubed
    		•
  • 1 scoop ube (purple yam) ice cream
    		•
  • Evaporated milk to taste
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Layer the ingredients
In a tall glass, layer the sweetened fruits, beans, and other ingredients.

Step 2 - Add shaved ice
Top with shaved ice.

Step 3 - Add leche flan and ice cream
Place a few cubes of leche flan and a scoop of ube ice cream on top.

Step 4 - Add evaporated milk
Pour evaporated milk over the ice.

Step 5 - Serve
Serve immediately. Mix well before eating. Enjoy your refreshing Halo Halo!
