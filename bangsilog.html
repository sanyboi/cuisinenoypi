Bangsilog


Ingredients:
  • 1 cup fried rice
    		•
  • 2 eggs, fried
    		•
  • 2 pieces bangus (milkfish), marinated and fried
    		•
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
    		•
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 1 onion, chopped
    		•
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, sliced
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons oil
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Marinate the Bangus
Marinate the bangus in vinegar, garlic, and ginger for at least 30 minutes.

Step 2 - Fry the Bangus
In a pan, heat the oil over medium heat. Fry the bangus until golden brown and crispy.

Step 3 - Cook the Fried Rice
In the same pan, saut√© garlic until golden brown. Add the cooked rice and stir-fry for a few minutes. Season with salt and pepper.

Step 4 - Fry the Eggs
Fry the eggs sunny side up or according to your preference.

Step 5 - Assemble the Bangsilog
On a plate, serve the fried rice, fried bangus, and fried eggs together. Enjoy your delicious Bangsilog!
