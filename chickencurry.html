Chicken Curry


Ingredients:
  • 1 kg chicken, cut into serving pieces
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons oil
    		•
  • 1 onion, chopped
    		•
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, sliced
    		•
  • 2 cups coconut milk
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
    		•
  • 2 medium potatoes, quartered
    		•
  • 1 large carrot, sliced
    		•
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
    		•
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce (patis)
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Sauté the Aromatics
In a large pot, heat the oil over medium heat. Add the garlic, onion, and ginger. Sauté until they are fragrant and translucent.

Step 2 - Cook the Chicken
Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are lightly browned.

Step 3 - Add the Curry Powder
Add the curry powder and cook for a few minutes, stirring to coat the chicken evenly.

Step 4 - Simmer
Pour in the coconut milk and fish sauce. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for about 10 minutes.

Step 5 - Add Vegetables
Add the potatoes and carrots. Continue to simmer until the vegetables are tender.

Step 6 - Add Bell Peppers
Add the red and green bell peppers. Cook for an additional 5 minutes.

Step 7 - Season
Season with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the seasoning as necessary.

Step 8 - Serve
Serve the Chicken Curry hot with steamed rice. Enjoy your delicious Chicken Curry!
