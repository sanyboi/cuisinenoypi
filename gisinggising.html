Gising Gising


Ingredients:
  • 2 cups sigarilyas (winged beans), sliced
    		•
  • 1 medium red onion, chopped
    		•
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
    		•
  • 1 cup ground pork
    		•
  • 2 cups coconut milk
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
    		•
  • 2 pieces red chili, chopped
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Sauté the aromatics
In a pan, sauté garlic, onions, and tomatoes until softened.

Step 2 - Cook the ground pork
Add the ground pork and cook until browned.

Step 3 - Add the sigarilyas
Add the sigarilyas and cook for a few minutes.

Step 4 - Add the coconut milk and seasonings
Pour in the coconut milk, add fish sauce, and season with salt and pepper. Add the chopped red chili and simmer for 10 minutes.

Step 5 - Serve
Serve hot with rice.
