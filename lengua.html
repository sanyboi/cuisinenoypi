Lengua


Ingredients:
  • 1 kg ox tongue (lengua)
    		•
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
    		•
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 2 bay leaves
    		•
  • 1 cup heavy cream
    		•
  • 1 cup beef broth
    		•
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons butter
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
  • Oil for frying
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Clean the ox tongue
In a pot, boil the ox tongue with enough water to cover, adding bay leaves and salt. Cook until tender, about 2-3 hours. Let it cool, then peel off the outer skin. Slice into thin pieces.

Step 2 - Sauté the aromatics
In a pan, heat oil and butter. Sauté garlic and onions until softened.

Step 3 - Cook the tongue
Add the sliced ox tongue and cook until lightly browned.

Step 4 - Add mushrooms and broth
Add the mushrooms, beef broth, and soy sauce. Simmer until the sauce reduces slightly.

Step 5 - Add the cream
Pour in the heavy cream and stir until well combined. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Simmer for another 10 minutes.

Step 6 - Serve
Serve hot with rice or mashed potatoes. Enjoy your Lengua!
