Kutsinta


Ingredients:
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
    		•
  • 1 cup brown sugar
    		•
  • 2 cups water
    		•
  • 1 teaspoon lye water (lihiya)
    		•
  • 1 teaspoon annatto powder (atsuete)
    		•
  • Grated coconut for topping
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Prepare the batter
In a bowl, combine flour, brown sugar, water, lye water, and annatto powder. Mix well until smooth.

Step 2 - Pour into molds
Pour the mixture into individual molds, filling each about 3/4 full.

Step 3 - Steam the kutsinta
Arrange the molds in a steamer and steam for 30-40 minutes or until set. Allow to cool.

Step 4 - Serve
Remove from molds and top with grated coconut. Enjoy your Kutsinta!
