Ginataang Manok


Ingredients:
  • 1 whole chicken, cut into serving pieces
    		•
  • 2 cups coconut milk
    		•
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, sliced
    		•
  • 1 medium red onion, chopped
    		•
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
    		•
  • 1 bunch malunggay leaves
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Sauté the aromatics
In a pot, sauté garlic, onions, and ginger until fragrant.

Step 2 - Add the chicken
Add the chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned.

Step 3 - Add the coconut milk
Pour in the coconut milk and bring to a boil. Simmer until the chicken is cooked through.

Step 4 - Add the malunggay leaves
Add the malunggay leaves and cook for another 2 minutes.

Step 5 - Season
Add fish sauce, salt, and pepper to taste. Serve hot with rice.
