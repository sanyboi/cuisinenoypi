Maja Blanca


Ingredients:
  • 2 cups coconut milk
    		•
  • 1 cup cornstarch
    		•
  • 1 cup fresh milk
    		•
  • 3/4 cup sugar
    		•
  • 1 cup whole kernel corn, drained
    		•
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese (optional)
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Prepare the mixture
In a large pot, combine coconut milk, fresh milk, and sugar. Stir well until the sugar is dissolved. Bring to a boil over medium heat.

Step 2 - Add the cornstarch
In a separate bowl, dissolve cornstarch in 1 cup of water. Gradually add the cornstarch mixture to the boiling milk mixture, stirring continuously to avoid lumps.

Step 3 - Add the corn
Add the whole kernel corn and continue stirring until the mixture thickens and becomes smooth.

Step 4 - Transfer to a mold
Pour the thickened mixture into a greased dish or mold. Smooth the top with a spatula.

Step 5 - Cool and serve
Allow the Maja Blanca to cool and set. You can refrigerate it for a firmer texture. Garnish with grated cheese if desired. Slice and serve. Enjoy your Maja Blanca!
