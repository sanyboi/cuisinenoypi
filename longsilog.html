Long Silog


Ingredients:
  • 6 pieces longganisa (Filipino sausage)
    		•
  • 4 cups cooked garlic fried rice (sinangag)
    		•
  • 4 eggs, cooked sunny-side up
    		•
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Cook the longganisa
In a frying pan, heat oil over medium heat. Cook the longganisa until fully cooked and browned on all sides. Set aside.

Step 2 - Prepare the garlic fried rice
In the same pan, saut√© minced garlic until golden brown. Add the cooked rice and stir well. Season with salt to taste. Set aside.

Step 3 - Cook the eggs
In a separate pan, cook the eggs sunny-side up. Set aside.

Step 4 - Assemble the Long Silog
On a plate, arrange a serving of garlic fried rice, longganisa, and a sunny-side up egg.

Step 5 - Serve
Serve hot with your favorite side dish or dipping sauce. Enjoy your Long Silog!
