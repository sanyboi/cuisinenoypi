Ginisang Sayote


Ingredients:
  • 3 pieces sayote (chayote), peeled and sliced
    		•
  • 1 medium red onion, chopped
    		•
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
    		•
  • 1/4 cup water
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Sauté the aromatics
In a pan, sauté garlic, onions, and tomatoes until softened.

Step 2 - Add the sayote
Add the sayote slices and cook for a few minutes until tender.

Step 3 - Add water and seasonings
Pour in water and add fish sauce. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Simmer for 5 minutes.

Step 4 - Serve
Serve hot with rice.
