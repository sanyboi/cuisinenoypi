Batangas Lomi


Ingredients:
  • 1 kg lomi noodles
    		•
  • 1/2 kg pork belly, sliced
    		•
  • 1/4 kg pork liver, sliced
    		•
  • 1 onion, chopped
    		•
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 1 carrot, julienned
    		•
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
    		•
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
    		•
  • 1 liter water
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
  • Spring onions for garnish
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Sauté the Aromatics
In a large pot, heat oil over medium heat. Sauté the garlic and onion until fragrant.

Step 2 - Add the Pork
Add the pork belly and liver, and cook until browned.

Step 3 - Add the Vegetables
Add the carrot and cook for another 3 minutes.

Step 4 - Add the Broth
Pour in the water, soy sauce, and fish sauce. Bring to a boil.

Step 5 - Add the Noodles
Add the lomi noodles and simmer until cooked. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 6 - Serve
Garnish with spring onions and serve hot. Enjoy your Batangas Lomi!
