Chicken Inasal


Ingredients:
  • 1 kg chicken thighs or drumsticks
    		•
  • 1/4 cup calamansi juice (or lemon juice)
    		•
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
    		•
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
    		•
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, minced
    		•
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
    		•
  • 1/4 cup annatto oil
    		•
  • 1 teaspoon salt
    		•
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Marinate the Chicken
In a large bowl, combine the calamansi juice, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, ginger, brown sugar, salt, and ground black pepper. Add the chicken pieces and marinate for at least 1 hour, preferably overnight, in the refrigerator.

Step 2 - Preheat the Grill
Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

Step 3 - Grill the Chicken
Remove the chicken from the marinade and let any excess drip off. Grill the chicken pieces for about 30-40 minutes, turning occasionally and basting with annatto oil, until the chicken is cooked through and has a nice char.

Step 4 - Serve
Transfer the grilled chicken to a serving platter. Serve hot with steamed rice, atchara (pickled papaya), and soy sauce with calamansi for dipping. Enjoy your delicious Chicken Inasal!
