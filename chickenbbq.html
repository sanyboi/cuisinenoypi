Chicken BBQ


Ingredients:
  • 1 kg chicken thighs or drumsticks
    		•
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
    		•
  • 1/4 cup calamansi juice (or lemon juice)
    		•
  • 1/4 cup banana ketchup
    		•
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
    		•
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
    		•
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil
    		•
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Marinate the Chicken
In a large bowl, combine the soy sauce, calamansi juice, banana ketchup, brown sugar, garlic, ground black pepper, and cooking oil. Add the chicken pieces and marinate for at least 1 hour, preferably overnight, in the refrigerator.

Step 2 - Preheat the Grill
Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

Step 3 - Grill the Chicken
Remove the chicken from the marinade and let any excess drip off. Grill the chicken pieces for about 30-40 minutes, turning occasionally and basting with the melted butter, until the chicken is cooked through and has a nice char.

Step 4 - Serve
Transfer the grilled chicken to a serving platter. Serve hot with steamed rice and atchara (pickled papaya). Enjoy your delicious Chicken BBQ!
