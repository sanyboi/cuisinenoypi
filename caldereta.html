Caldereta


Ingredients:
  • 1 kg beef or goat, cut into cubes
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons oil
    		•
  • 1 onion, chopped
    		•
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 2 cups beef broth
    		•
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
    		•
  • 1/4 cup liver spread or liver pate
    		•
  • 2 large potatoes, quartered
    		•
  • 2 large carrots, sliced
    		•
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
    		•
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
    		•
  • 1/4 cup green peas
    		•
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Sauté the Aromatics
In a large pot, heat the oil over medium heat. Add the garlic and onion and sauté until they are translucent.

Step 2 - Brown the Meat
Add the beef or goat meat and cook until it is browned on all sides.

Step 3 - Simmer
Add the beef broth and tomato sauce. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for about 1.5 to 2 hours or until the meat is tender.

Step 4 - Add Vegetables and Liver Spread
Add the liver spread, potatoes, carrots, and bell peppers. Cook for about 10-15 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.

Step 5 - Add Green Peas and Cheese
Add the green peas and grated cheese. Stir well and cook for an additional 5 minutes.

Step 6 - Season
Season with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the seasoning as necessary.

Step 7 - Serve
Serve the Caldereta hot with steamed rice. Enjoy your delicious Caldereta!
