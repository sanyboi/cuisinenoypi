Insarabasab


Ingredients:
  • 1 kg pork belly (liempo), grilled and sliced
    		•
  • 1 medium red onion, chopped
    		•
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
    		•
  • 1 tablespoon calamansi juice
    		•
  • 2 pieces red chili, chopped
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Grill the pork belly
Grill the pork belly until fully cooked and slightly charred. Slice into bite-sized pieces.

Step 2 - Sauté the aromatics
In a pan, sauté garlic and onion until softened.

Step 3 - Combine the ingredients
Add the sliced pork belly to the pan. Pour in soy sauce, vinegar, and calamansi juice. Add chopped red chili and mix well.

Step 4 - Season
Season with salt and pepper to taste. Cook for another 5 minutes.

Step 5 - Serve
Serve hot with rice. Enjoy your Insarabasab!
