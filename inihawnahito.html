Inihaw na Hito


Ingredients:
  • 2 large hito (catfish), cleaned and gutted
    		•
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons calamansi juice
    		•
  • 1 medium red onion, chopped
    		•
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, minced
    		•
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
  • Banana leaves for wrapping
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Prepare the marinade
In a bowl, combine soy sauce, calamansi juice, onion, garlic, ginger, and brown sugar. Season with salt and pepper. Mix well.

Step 2 - Marinate the hito
Marinate the hito in the mixture for at least 30 minutes.

Step 3 - Wrap in banana leaves
Wrap the marinated hito in banana leaves.

Step 4 - Grill the hito
Grill over medium heat for 20-25 minutes on each side or until the fish is cooked through.

Step 5 - Serve
Serve hot with rice and dipping sauce. Enjoy your Inihaw na Hito!
