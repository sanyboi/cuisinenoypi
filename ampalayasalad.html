Ampalaya Salad


Ingredients:
  • 2 pieces bitter melon (ampalaya), thinly sliced
    		•
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
    		•
  • 2 tomatoes, sliced
    		•
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
    		•
  • 1/4 cup water
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Prepare the Ampalaya
In a bowl, mix the ampalaya slices with 1 tablespoon of salt. Let it sit for 10 minutes to draw out the bitterness, then rinse thoroughly with cold water.

Step 2 - Mix the Vegetables
In a large bowl, combine the rinsed ampalaya, red onion, and tomatoes.

Step 3 - Prepare the Dressing
In a small bowl, mix together the vinegar, water, sugar, salt, and pepper until well combined.

Step 4 - Toss the Salad
Pour the dressing over the vegetable mixture and toss until evenly coated.

Step 5 - Chill and Serve
Chill the salad in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before serving. Enjoy your refreshing Ampalaya Salad!
