Ensaladang Talong


Ingredients:
  • 4 medium-sized eggplants, grilled and peeled
    		•
  • 2 large tomatoes, cored and diced
    		•
  • 1 medium red onion, minced
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons bagoong (fermented shrimp paste)
    		•
  • (Optional) Salted egg, quartered
    		•
Dressing (choose one):
  • ¼ cup white vinegar
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon granulated white sugar

  • OR

    • Calamansi or lemon juice
    • (Optional) Soy sauce, to taste
    Method:
    Step 1 - Prepare the Ingredients
    Combine the grilled and peeled eggplants, tomatoes, and onions in a bowl.

    Step 2 - Add your chosen dressing
    If using vinegar, mix the vinegar, black pepper, salt, and sugar in a separate bowl. Pour over the salad and toss gently to coat.

    If using calamansi or lemon juice, add to the salad according to your taste preference. You can also add a little soy sauce for additional flavor (optional).

    Step 3 - Add optional ingredients (optional)
    Gently fold in the bagoong and/or salted egg if using.

    Step 4 - Serve
    Serve immediately. Enjoy your healthy and refreshing Ensaladang Talong!
