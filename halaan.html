Halaan


Ingredients:
  • 1 kg halaan (clams), cleaned
    		•
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, sliced
    		•
  • 1 medium red onion, chopped
    		•
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 2 cups water
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
    		•
  • 1 bunch spinach or malunggay leaves
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Sauté the aromatics
In a pot, sauté garlic, onions, and ginger until fragrant.

Step 2 - Add the clams
Add the cleaned clams and cook for a few minutes.

Step 3 - Add water and seasonings
Pour in water and add fish sauce. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Bring to a boil and simmer until the clams open.

Step 4 - Add the greens
Add the spinach or malunggay leaves and cook for another 2 minutes.

Step 5 - Serve
Serve hot with rice.
