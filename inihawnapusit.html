Inihaw na Pusit


Ingredients:
  • 4 large squid (pusit), cleaned
    		•
  • 1 medium red onion, chopped
    		•
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
    		•
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons calamansi juice
    		•
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
  • Banana leaves for wrapping (optional)
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Prepare the stuffing
In a bowl, combine onion, tomatoes, garlic, soy sauce, calamansi juice, and brown sugar. Season with salt and pepper. Mix well.

Step 2 - Stuff the squid
Stuff the squid with the mixture. Secure with toothpicks if necessary.

Step 3 - Grill the squid
Grill over medium heat for 3-5 minutes on each side or until cooked through. Do not overcook to avoid toughness.

Step 4 - Serve
Serve hot with rice and dipping sauce. Enjoy your Inihaw na Pusit!
