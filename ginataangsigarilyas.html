Ginataang Sigarilyas


Ingredients:
  • 4 cups sigarilyas (winged beans), cut into 2-inch pieces
    		•
  • 2 cups coconut milk
    		•
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, sliced
    		•
  • 1 medium red onion, chopped
    		•
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Sauté the aromatics
In a pot, sauté garlic, onions, and ginger until fragrant.

Step 2 - Add the sigarilyas
Add the sigarilyas and cook for a few minutes.

Step 3 - Add the coconut milk
Pour in the coconut milk and bring to a boil. Simmer until the sigarilyas is tender.

Step 4 - Season
Add fish sauce, salt, and pepper to taste. Serve hot with rice.
