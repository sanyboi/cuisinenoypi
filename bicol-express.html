Bicol Express


Ingredients:
  • 1 kg pork belly, sliced into strips
    		•
  • 2 cups coconut milk
    		•
  • 1 cup coconut cream
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons shrimp paste (bagoong)
    		•
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 1 onion, chopped
    		•
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
    		•
  • 3-4 green chilies, sliced
    		•
  • 3-4 red chilies, sliced
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Prepare the Ingredients
Slice the pork belly into strips. Mince the garlic, chop the onion, and slice the green and red chilies. Set aside.

Step 2 - Saute the Aromatics
In a large pan, heat the cooking oil over medium heat. Saute the garlic and onion until fragrant and the onion becomes translucent.

Step 3 - Cook the Pork
Add the pork belly to the pan and cook until it turns light brown. Stir occasionally to ensure even cooking.

Step 4 - Add the Shrimp Paste
Add the shrimp paste (bagoong) to the pan. Stir well to combine and cook for about 2-3 minutes.

Step 5 - Pour in the Coconut Milk
Pour in the coconut milk and bring the mixture to a boil. Reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 20-30 minutes, or until the pork is tender.

Step 6 - Add the Chilies and Coconut Cream
Add the sliced green and red chilies to the pan. Pour in the coconut cream and continue to simmer for another 10 minutes, or until the sauce thickens to your desired consistency.

Step 7 - Season and Serve
Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot with steamed rice. Enjoy your delicious Bicol Express!
