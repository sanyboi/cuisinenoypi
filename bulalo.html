Bulalo


Ingredients:
  • 1.5 kg beef shank with bone marrow
    		•
  • 2 liters water
    		•
  • 1 onion, quartered
    		•
  • 1 tablespoon whole black peppercorns
    		•
  • 2-3 pieces corn on the cob, cut into chunks
    		•
  • 2-3 potatoes, quartered
    		•
  • 1 head cabbage, quartered
    		•
  • 1 bunch green beans
    		•
  • Salt to taste
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Boil the Beef
In a large pot, combine the beef shank, water, onion, and peppercorns. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for about 1.5 to 2 hours or until the beef is tender. Skim off any scum that rises to the surface.

Step 2 - Add Vegetables
Add the corn and potatoes to the pot. Continue to simmer until the vegetables are tender, about 20-30 minutes.

Step 3 - Add Remaining Vegetables
Add the cabbage and green beans. Cook for an additional 10-15 minutes or until the vegetables are cooked to your liking.

Step 4 - Season
Season the broth with salt to taste. Adjust the seasoning as necessary.

Step 5 - Serve
Serve the Bulalo hot, with steamed rice and a dipping sauce of fish sauce with calamansi or lemon. Enjoy your delicious Bulalo!
