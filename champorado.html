Champorado


Ingredients:
  • 1 cup glutinous rice (malagkit)
    		•
  • 4 cups water
    		•
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
    		•
  • 1/2 cup sugar
    		•
  • 1/2 cup evaporated milk
    		•
  • 1/2 cup condensed milk (for topping)
    		•
  • Pinch of salt
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Cook the Rice
In a large pot, combine the glutinous rice and water. Bring to a boil over medium heat, then reduce the heat and let it simmer until the rice is cooked, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.

Step 2 - Add Cocoa and Sugar
Add the cocoa powder and sugar to the pot. Stir well until the cocoa and sugar are fully dissolved and the mixture is well combined. Add a pinch of salt to enhance the flavor.

Step 3 - Simmer
Continue to simmer the mixture until it thickens to your desired consistency, stirring occasionally.

Step 4 - Serve
Ladle the Champorado into bowls. Drizzle with evaporated milk and top with condensed milk as desired. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Champorado!
