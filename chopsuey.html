Chopsuey


Ingredients:
  • 1/2 kg chicken breast or pork, sliced thinly
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons oil
    		•
  • 1 onion, chopped
    		•
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 1 cup cauliflower florets
    		•
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
    		•
  • 1 carrot, sliced
    		•
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
    		•
  • 1 cup snow peas
    		•
  • 1 cup cabbage, chopped
    		•
  • 1 cup quail eggs, boiled and peeled (optional)
    		•
  • 1 cup chicken or beef broth
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    		•
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
    		•
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch dissolved in 1/4 cup water
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Sauté the Aromatics
In a large pan or wok, heat the oil over medium heat. Add the garlic and onion and sauté until they are fragrant and translucent.

Step 2 - Cook the Meat
Add the chicken or pork slices and cook until they are browned and cooked through.

Step 3 - Add Vegetables
Add the cauliflower, broccoli, carrot, bell pepper, and snow peas. Stir-fry for a few minutes until the vegetables are tender but still crisp.

Step 4 - Add Cabbage and Quail Eggs
Add the cabbage and quail eggs (if using). Stir well to combine.

Step 5 - Add Broth and Sauces
Pour in the chicken or beef broth, soy sauce, and oyster sauce. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for a few minutes.

Step 6 - Thicken the Sauce
Add the dissolved cornstarch and cook, stirring constantly, until the sauce thickens.

Step 7 - Season
Season with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the seasoning as necessary.

Step 8 - Serve
Transfer the Chopsuey to a serving dish. Serve hot with steamed rice. Enjoy your delicious Chopsuey!
