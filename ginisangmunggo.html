Ginisang Munggo


Ingredients:
  • 1 cup mung beans, washed and soaked
    		•
  • 4 cups water
    		•
  • 1 medium red onion, chopped
    		•
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
    		•
  • 1 cup malunggay leaves (moringa)
    		•
  • 1/4 cup pork, diced (optional)
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Cook the mung beans
In a pot, combine mung beans and water. Bring to a boil and simmer until the beans are tender.

Step 2 - Sauté the aromatics
In a pan, sauté garlic, onions, and tomatoes until softened. Add the pork if using and cook until browned.

Step 3 - Combine and season
Add the sautéed mixture to the cooked mung beans. Add fish sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for another 10 minutes.

Step 4 - Add the malunggay
Stir in the malunggay leaves and cook for another 2 minutes.

Step 5 - Serve
Serve hot with rice.
