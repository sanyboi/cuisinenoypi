Ginataang Mais


Ingredients:
  • 2 cups corn kernels
    		•
  • 1 cup glutinous rice
    		•
  • 4 cups coconut milk
    		•
  • 1 cup sugar
    		•
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Cook the rice
In a pot, combine glutinous rice and 2 cups of water. Cook until the rice is tender.

Step 2 - Add the coconut milk and corn
Pour in the coconut milk, add the corn kernels, and bring to a boil. Simmer for 10 minutes.

Step 3 - Add sugar and salt
Add sugar and salt. Stir and cook for another 5 minutes. Serve hot or cold.
