Fish Escabeche


Ingredients:
  • 1 whole fish (tilapia or lapu-lapu), cleaned and scored
    		•
  • 1 large carrot, julienned
    		•
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced into strips
    		•
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced into strips
    		•
  • 1 medium red onion, sliced
    		•
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, julienned
    		•
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
    		•
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
    		•
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
    		•
  • 1/2 cup water
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Fry the fish
Fry the fish in hot oil until golden brown. Set aside.

Step 2 - Sauté the aromatics
In a pan, sauté garlic, onions, and ginger until fragrant.

Step 3 - Add the vegetables
Add carrots and bell peppers. Cook until slightly tender.

Step 4 - Add the liquid ingredients
Pour in vinegar, soy sauce, water, and brown sugar. Simmer until the sauce thickens slightly.

Step 5 - Combine
Place the fried fish on a serving platter and pour the sauce and vegetables over the fish. Serve hot.
