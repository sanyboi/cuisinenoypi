Adobong Baboy


Ingredients:
  • 1 kg pork belly, cut into cubes
    		•
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
    		•
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
    		•
  • 1 head garlic, minced
    		•
  • 1 onion, chopped
    		•
  • 2 bay leaves
    		•
  • 1 teaspoon peppercorns
    		•
  • 2 potatoes, quartered (optional)
    		•
  • 1/4 cup oil
    		•
  • 1 cup water
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (optional)
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Marinate the Pork
In a large bowl, combine the pork belly, soy sauce, and garlic. Mix well and marinate for at least 30 minutes.

Step 2 - Sauté the Aromatics
In a large pot, heat the oil over medium heat. Add the onions and sauté until they are translucent. Add the marinated pork and cook until it is browned on all sides.

Step 3 - Simmer the Adobo
Add the vinegar, water, bay leaves, and peppercorns to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 1 hour, or until the pork is tender. If using potatoes, add them halfway through the cooking time.

Step 4 - Adjust Seasoning
Add sugar if desired and season with salt and pepper to taste. Continue to simmer until the sauce has thickened.

Step 5 - Serve
Serve the adobo hot with steamed rice. Enjoy your delicious Adobong Baboy!
