Leche Flan


Ingredients:
  • 10 egg yolks
    		•
  • 1 can (14 oz) condensed milk
    		•
  • 1 can (12 oz) evaporated milk
    		•
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    		•
  • 1 cup sugar
    		•
  • 1/4 cup water
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Make the caramel
In a saucepan, combine sugar and water. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the sugar caramelizes. Pour the caramel into llaneras (molds) and spread evenly. Let it cool and harden.

Step 2 - Prepare the flan mixture
In a bowl, combine egg yolks, condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla extract. Mix well until smooth.

Step 3 - Pour the mixture into molds
Pour the flan mixture over the hardened caramel in the llaneras.

Step 4 - Steam the flan
Cover the molds with aluminum foil and steam for 30-40 minutes or until set. You can also bake it in a water bath at 350°F (175°C) for 45-50 minutes.

Step 5 - Cool and serve
Let the flan cool to room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 2 hours. To serve, run a knife around the edges and invert onto a plate. Enjoy your Leche Flan!
