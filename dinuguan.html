Dinuguan


Ingredients:
  • 1 kg pork belly or pork shoulder, cut into cubes
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons oil
    		•
  • 1 onion, chopped
    		•
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 2 cups pork blood, fresh or frozen, thawed
    		•
  • 1 cup vinegar
    		•
  • 2 cups water
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce (patis)
    		•
  • 2 pieces long green chili (siling haba), sliced
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Sauté the Aromatics
In a large pot, heat the oil over medium heat. Add the garlic and onion and sauté until they are fragrant and translucent.

Step 2 - Cook the Pork
Add the pork cubes and cook until they are browned on all sides.

Step 3 - Add Vinegar and Water
Add the vinegar and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for about 30 minutes or until the pork is tender.

Step 4 - Add Pork Blood
Slowly add the pork blood while stirring continuously to avoid curdling. Continue to simmer for about 10-15 minutes.

Step 5 - Season and Add Chili
Add the fish sauce, long green chili, salt, and pepper. Stir well and cook for another 5 minutes.

Step 6 - Serve
Transfer the Dinuguan to a serving bowl. Serve hot with steamed rice or puto (rice cake). Enjoy your delicious Dinuguan!
