Inihaw na Manok


Ingredients:
  • 1 whole chicken, cut into serving pieces
    		•
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
    		•
  • 1/4 cup calamansi juice
    		•
  • 1/4 cup banana ketchup
    		•
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 1 medium red onion, chopped
    		•
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
    		•
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
    		•
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Prepare the marinade
In a bowl, combine soy sauce, calamansi juice, banana ketchup, garlic, onion, brown sugar, ground black pepper, and salt. Mix well.

Step 2 - Marinate the chicken
Marinate the chicken pieces in the mixture for at least 1 hour.

Step 3 - Grill the chicken
Grill over medium heat for 20-25 minutes on each side or until fully cooked and slightly charred.

Step 4 - Serve
Serve hot with rice and dipping sauce. Enjoy your Inihaw na Manok!
