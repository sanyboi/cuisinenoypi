Kinunot na Pagi


Ingredients:
  • 1 kg pagi (stingray), cleaned and cut into pieces
    		•
  • 2 cups coconut milk
    		•
  • 1 cup malunggay leaves
    		•
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, sliced
    		•
  • 1 medium red onion, chopped
    		•
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
    		•
  • 2 pieces red chili, chopped
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Boil the stingray
In a pot, boil the stingray in water with ginger until tender. Drain and shred the meat. Set aside.

Step 2 - Sauté the aromatics
In a pan, sauté garlic, onion, and ginger until fragrant.

Step 3 - Add the coconut milk
Pour in the coconut milk and bring to a boil. Simmer until it thickens.

Step 4 - Add the shredded stingray
Add the shredded stingray, vinegar, and red chili. Season with salt and pepper. Simmer for another 10 minutes.

Step 5 - Add the malunggay leaves
Add the malunggay leaves and cook for another 2 minutes.

Step 6 - Serve
Serve hot with rice. Enjoy your Kinunot na Pagi!
