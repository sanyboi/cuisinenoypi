Batangas Lomi


Ingredients:
  • 500 grams fresh egg noodles
    		•
  • 1/4 kg pork belly, sliced into thin strips
    		•
  • 1/4 kg pork liver, sliced into thin strips
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    		•
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 1 medium red onion, chopped
    		•
  • 1 medium carrot, julienned
    		•
  • 1 small cabbage, shredded
    		•
  • 1/4 cup flour diluted in 1/4 cup water
    		•
  • 6 cups pork broth
    		•
  • 2 eggs, beaten
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
  • Spring onions for garnish
    		•
  • Fish sauce to taste
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Sauté the aromatics
In a large pot, sauté garlic and onions until fragrant.

Step 2 - Cook the pork belly
Add the pork belly and cook until browned. Add the pork liver and soy sauce, and cook for another 5 minutes.

Step 3 - Add the broth
Pour in the pork broth and bring to a boil.

Step 4 - Add the noodles and vegetables
Add the fresh egg noodles, carrots, and cabbage. Cook until the noodles and vegetables are tender.

Step 5 - Thicken the soup
Gradually add the diluted flour mixture while stirring constantly until the soup thickens.

Step 6 - Add the eggs
Slowly pour in the beaten eggs while stirring to create egg ribbons.

Step 7 - Season and serve
Season with salt, pepper, and fish sauce to taste. Garnish with spring onions. Serve hot. Enjoy your Lomi!
