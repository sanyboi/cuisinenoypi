Bistek Tagalog


Ingredients:
  • 1 kg beef sirloin, thinly sliced
    		•
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
    		•
  • 1/2 cup calamansi juice (or lemon juice)
    		•
  • 1 head garlic, minced
    		•
  • 1 large onion, sliced into rings
    		•
  • 1/4 cup oil
    		•
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
    		•
  • 1 cup water
    		•
  • Salt to taste
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Marinate the Beef
In a large bowl, combine the beef, soy sauce, calamansi juice, and garlic. Marinate for at least 30 minutes or overnight for best results.

Step 2 - Saut√© the Onions
In a large pan, heat the oil over medium heat. Add the onion rings and cook until they are soft and translucent. Remove the onions from the pan and set aside.

Step 3 - Cook the Beef
In the same pan, add the marinated beef (reserve the marinade) and cook until it is browned on all sides.

Step 4 - Simmer the Sauce
Add the marinade and water to the pan. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 20-30 minutes or until the beef is tender.

Step 5 - Season
Season with ground black pepper and salt to taste. Continue to simmer until the sauce has thickened to your liking.

Step 6 - Serve
Return the cooked onions to the pan and mix well. Serve the Bistek Tagalog hot with steamed rice. Enjoy your delicious Bistek Tagalog!
