Kalamay


Ingredients:
  • 2 cups glutinous rice flour
    		•
  • 1 1/2 cups coconut milk
    		•
  • 1 cup brown sugar
    		•
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    		•
  • 1 cup grated coconut for topping
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Make the batter
In a bowl, combine glutinous rice flour, coconut milk, brown sugar, and salt. Mix well until smooth.

Step 2 - Cook the mixture
Transfer the mixture to a non-stick pan and cook over low heat, stirring continuously until thick and smooth.

Step 3 - Transfer to a mold
Pour the cooked mixture into a greased mold or tray. Smooth the top with a spatula.

Step 4 - Add toppings
Top with grated coconut.

Step 5 - Serve
Let it cool before slicing. Serve and enjoy your Kalamay!
