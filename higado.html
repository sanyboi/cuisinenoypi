Higado


Ingredients:
  • 500 grams pork liver, sliced thinly
    		•
  • 500 grams pork tenderloin, sliced thinly
    		•
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
    		•
  • 1/4 cup calamansi juice
    		•
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
    		•
  • 1 medium red onion, chopped
    		•
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
    		•
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
    		•
  • 1 cup water
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Marinate the pork liver and tenderloin
In a bowl, combine soy sauce, calamansi juice, and vinegar. Marinate the pork liver and tenderloin slices for at least 30 minutes.

Step 2 - Sauté the aromatics
In a pan, sauté garlic, onions, and tomatoes until softened.

Step 3 - Cook the pork tenderloin
Add the marinated pork tenderloin and cook until browned.

Step 4 - Add the pork liver
Add the pork liver and cook until no longer pink.

Step 5 - Add water and seasonings
Pour in water and add bell pepper. Season with salt and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes or until the sauce thickens.

Step 6 - Serve
Serve hot with rice. Enjoy your Higado!
