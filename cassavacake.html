Cassava Cake


Ingredients:
  • 2 cups grated cassava
    		•
  • 1 cup coconut milk
    		•
  • 1 cup condensed milk
    		•
  • 1/2 cup evaporated milk
    		•
  • 1/2 cup sugar
    		•
  • 2 eggs, beaten
    		•
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese
    		•
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    		•
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
    		•
  • 1/2 cup coconut cream (for topping)
    		•
  • 1/2 cup condensed milk (for topping)
    		•
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese (for topping)
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Preheat Oven
Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).

Step 2 - Mix Ingredients
In a large mixing bowl, combine the grated cassava, coconut milk, condensed milk, evaporated milk, sugar, eggs, grated cheese, vanilla extract, and melted butter. Mix well until all ingredients are well combined.

Step 3 - Bake
Pour the mixture into a greased baking dish. Bake for about 45 minutes to 1 hour or until the top is golden brown and a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.

Step 4 - Prepare Topping
In a small pot, combine the coconut cream and condensed milk. Cook over medium heat, stirring continuously until the mixture thickens.

Step 5 - Add Topping
Pour the topping mixture over the baked cassava cake. Sprinkle with grated cheese. Return to the oven and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes or until the topping is set and slightly browned.

Step 6 - Cool and Serve
Let the cassava cake cool before slicing into squares. Serve and enjoy your delicious Cassava Cake!
