Inihaw na Bangus


Ingredients:
  • 1 large bangus (milkfish), cleaned and butterflied
    		•
  • 2 medium tomatoes, diced
    		•
  • 1 medium red onion, chopped
    		•
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    		•
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, minced
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons calamansi juice
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
  • Banana leaves for wrapping
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Prepare the stuffing
In a bowl, combine tomatoes, onion, garlic, ginger, soy sauce, and calamansi juice. Season with salt and pepper. Mix well.

Step 2 - Stuff the bangus
Stuff the bangus with the tomato mixture. Secure with kitchen twine if necessary.

Step 3 - Wrap in banana leaves
Wrap the stuffed bangus in banana leaves.

Step 4 - Grill the bangus
Grill over medium heat for 15-20 minutes on each side or until the fish is cooked through.

Step 5 - Serve
Serve hot with rice and dipping sauce. Enjoy your Inihaw na Bangus!
