Dinengdeng


Ingredients:
  • 1/2 kg mixed vegetables (okra, eggplant, bitter melon, squash, string beans)
    		•
  • 2 cups water
    		•
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
    		•
  • 2 tomatoes, quartered
    		•
  • 1 cup saluyot leaves (jute leaves)
    		•
  • 1 cup malunggay leaves (moringa leaves)
    		•
  • 2 tablespoons bagoong (fermented fish paste)
    		•
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    		•
  • Grilled or fried fish (optional, for added flavor)
    		•
Method:
Step 1 - Boil Water
In a large pot, bring the water to a boil.

Step 2 - Add Aromatics
Add the onion and tomatoes to the boiling water. Let it boil for a few minutes until the tomatoes are soft.

Step 3 - Add Vegetables
Add the mixed vegetables (okra, eggplant, bitter melon, squash, string beans) to the pot. Cook until the vegetables are tender.

Step 4 - Add Leaves and Bagoong
Add the saluyot leaves, malunggay leaves, and bagoong. Stir well and let it simmer for a few more minutes.

Step 5 - Add Grilled or Fried Fish (Optional)
If using, add the grilled or fried fish to the pot for added flavor. Let it simmer for another 5 minutes.

Step 6 - Season
Season with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the seasoning as necessary.

Step 7 - Serve
Transfer the Dinengdeng to a serving bowl. Serve hot with steamed rice. Enjoy your delicious Dinengdeng!
